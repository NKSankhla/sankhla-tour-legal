Account & Data Deletion Policy

Last Updated: 27 February 2026

This policy explains how users of Sankhla Tour can request deletion of their account and personal data.

1. Account Deletion Request

Users may request deletion of their account by sending an email to the contact address listed below. The request must include the registered email address or mobile number used in the App.

2. Data That Will Be Deleted

Upon verification of the request, we will delete:

  • User profile information (name, email, phone number)
  • Stored booking history within the application

3. Data That May Be Retained

We may retain certain information if required for legal, tax, accounting, or regulatory compliance purposes.
